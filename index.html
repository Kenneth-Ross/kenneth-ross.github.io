About Me

Discover my journey in problem solving and design

I often find my interests stuck between the desire to learn Software, Hardware, and Mechanical design; I find my self at the intersection of the three!

Starting with a background in Roblox Game Development, which taught me everything from creative design, optimization, client-server communication, memory management, and the ability to foresee large projects in a systems level thinking. My scope of projects span from personal exploration all the way to Freelance Work. I can proudly say the skills I aquired from Roblox translate to all other problem solving domains.

Taking up the challenge of a World-Level Energy Competition; I learned about my love for Mechanical Design; 3D Printing, Gearboxes, and Wind Turbine Blades were the mediums through which I learned to hone my CAD skills. Being able to envision a design then holding it's final product is one of my greatest joys!

Now as a current Robotics Engineering Undergraduate Student at U.C Riverside; I find myself as an Associate Engineer in Highlander Racing Formula SAE with 100% ownership projects in an interdisciplinary start-up environment where I get to fully apply my interesction of interests in Software, Hardware, and Mechanical Design; Furthur cementing my passion to solve problems with creative solutions through engineering.
