About Me

Hello! I'm Kenneth Ross, and I study Robotics Engineering at University Of California, Riverside.

I specialize in controls and embedded systems, with foundations in Game Development, and Mechanical Design. As an Associate Engineer at Highlander Racing Formula SAE, I own full responsibility of our Autonomous Braking System's design and integration into our overall Guidance, Navigation, and Controls architecture.

Apart from my responsibilities with controls systems, I also create internal tooling software for our 3D Printing Fleet integrated into Discord, and a Tool Tracking system aimed at increasing tool retention and organization, while minimizing bureaucracy.
